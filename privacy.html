Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to PlayMazle.com. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect Automatically

We use third-party services like Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our game (e.g., page views, session duration, and general geographic location). This data is anonymized and helps us improve the game.

2. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3. Local Storage (Game Data)

To provide a seamless experience, we save your game progress, daily streak, and preferences (like dark/light mode and language) directly on your device using your browser's Local Storage. We do not store this data on our external servers.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@playmazle.com
