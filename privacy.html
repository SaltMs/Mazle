Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Welcome to PlayMazle. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data when you play our game on our website (PlayMazle.com) and our mobile applications (iOS and Android).

1. Data We Collect & Leaderboards (Firebase)

To provide a competitive "Daily Leaderboard" experience in our mobile apps, we securely transmit and store anonymous gameplay data on our external servers (Google Firebase/Firestore). This data includes:

  • An anonymous, randomly generated User ID.
  • Your Country Code (detected locally to display your country's flag).
  • Your puzzle completion time, score, and movement history.

We do not collect, store, or ask for any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your real name, email address, or phone number for the leaderboard.

2. Local Storage (Game Progress)

To provide a seamless experience, we save your offline game progress, daily streak, and preferences (like dark/light mode and sound settings) directly on your device using your device's Local Storage / SharedPreferences.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob & AdSense)

We use Google AdMob (for mobile apps) and Google AdSense (for the web) to display ads. Google and its partners use technologies like cookies and mobile advertising IDs (such as Apple's IDFA or Android's Advertising ID) to serve ads based on your prior interactions.

For iOS Users: We will request your permission to use your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) identifier to provide you with personalized ads. You can allow or deny this request. If you deny it, you will still see ads, but they will be less relevant to you.

You can opt out of personalized advertising globally by visiting Google's Ads Settings or adjusting your device's privacy settings.

4. Analytics

We may use third-party services like Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our game (e.g., session duration, crash reports). This data is anonymized and helps us improve the game's stability.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@playmazle.com
